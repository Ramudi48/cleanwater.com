Water Quality Testing and Monitoring

Monitoring the quality of water sources and treated water is crucial. This involves regular testing for parameters such as pH, turbidity, dissolved oxygen, heavy metals, and microbial content to ensure compliance with safety standards.,

Wastewater Treatment

Wastewater treatment involves processes to remove contaminants from sewage or industrial wastewater before it's discharged into the environment or reused. Treatment methods include physical, biological, and chemical processes like primary and secondary treatment, activated sludge processes, and advanced oxidation.

Water Recycling and Reuse

With increasing water scarcity, recycling and reusing water have become essential. Technologies such as membrane filtration, reverse osmosis, and advanced oxidation processes are used to treat wastewater for reuse in industrial processes, irrigation, or even drinking water supplies after suitable treatment.
